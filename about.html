About Bullpen Tracker

Bullpen Tracker was originally made as a utility for my college baseball team. However it quickly became a project that I wanted to release to the public. I am hoping to have the iOS app released to the app store and the website fully functional by the end of the calendar year.

The app and website were both made from the ground up by Sam Hollenbach, with some additional help from Andy Hayes. Please contact me at samhollenbach@gmail.com if you have questions, ideas, inquiries or want to learn more!
